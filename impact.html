Impact Stories

Our projects demonstrate how strategic planning, community engagement and resilience deliver tangible results. Explore case studies from infrastructure development, security and corporate transformation to see our values in action.

Case Studies

Setraco: Building Bridges

Founded in 1977 as a small construction firm, Setraco has grown into one of Nigeria’s leading civil engineering companies. Its portfolio spans major road and bridge projects, including the East–West Road, Kano–Maiduguri Highway and the Ikom Bridge[1][2]. Setraco’s emphasis on high‑quality materials and cutting‑edge technology illustrates how long‑term investment in infrastructure transforms communities.

OML 40: Securing Critical Assets

Ocean Marine Solutions (OMS) was tasked with securing and retrofitting the OML 40 pipeline in Nigeria. The company repaired all identified valve insertion points, engaged communities along the right‑of‑way and clamped over 100 illegal refineries. These efforts reduced oil losses from 62 % in December 2018 to 2 % by February 2019[3].

Eterna Plc: Driving Growth

Eterna Plc, an integrated energy company, recorded a 71 % revenue increase from ₦183.3 billion in 2023 to ₦313.6 billion in 2024, with profit before tax of ₦4.48 billion[4][5]. The company attributes this turnaround to strong leadership and strategic focus across retail marketing, aviation fuel, lubricants, LPG and other energy services. This demonstrates how diversified operations and sound governance can revitalise a business.

UN Early Warning & Response

In Kaduna and Katsina states, the United Nations and local partners implemented an Early Warning and Early Response (EWER) system to prevent conflict. Since August 2023 the system has recorded 126 cases, resolving 53 within three months[6]. By fostering collaboration among youth, security agencies and government, the initiative empowers communities to anticipate and de‑escalate tensions.
